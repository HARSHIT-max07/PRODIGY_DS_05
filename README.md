# PRODIGY_DS_05
# Traffic Accident Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **traffic accident data** to identify patterns related to **road conditions, weather, and time of day**. The goal is to uncover accident hotspots and contributing factors to improve **road safety and decision-making**.

---

## Business Objective

Traffic accidents cause significant safety and economic issues. This project aims to answer key questions:

* When do most accidents occur?
* Which weather conditions lead to more accidents?
* What road conditions are most risky?
* Where are accident hotspots located?
* What factors contribute most to accidents?

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes accident-related information such as:

* Date and Time of Accident
* Weather Conditions
* Road Conditions
* Location / Area
* Severity of Accident
* Traffic Conditions
* Light Conditions (Day/Night)

## Visualizations Created

* Accident frequency by time of day
* Weather vs accident count

---

## Key Insights

* Peak accidents occur during **evening and rush hours**
* **Rainy and foggy weather** increases accident probability
* **Poor road conditions** contribute to higher accident severity
* Identified **accident hotspot locations** with high frequency
* Night-time driving shows higher accident severity
